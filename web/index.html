What We Offer

Everything in One Platform

Replacing outdated manual processes with a smart, transparent, and efficient system.

Summons
Digital Offense Recording

Patrol staff can instantly record student and vehicle offenses on-site, replacing slow paper-based documentation.

Payments
Online Fine Settlement

Students can pay fines anytime online using a secure payment gateway — no more in-person office visits required.

Appeals
Transparent Appeal Process

Students submit appeals online. Clerical staff review and decide — keeping everyone informed with real-time status updates.

Vehicles
Vehicle Registration

Students register their vehicles digitally. Third-offense parking violations trigger automatic clamping enforcement.

Analytics
Predictive Insights

Data-driven dashboards identify offense hotspots, trends, and repeat offenders — enabling proactive campus governance.

Security
Role-Based Access

Separate portals for Students, Patrol Staff, and Clerical Staff — each with tailored tools and access controls.

Process

How It Works

Simple, transparent, and fast — from offense to resolution.

1
Offense Recorded

Patrol staff digitally reports a student or vehicle offense on-site with photo evidence and offense details.

2
Student Notified

The student receives the summons in their dashboard — with full details, fine amount, and available actions.

3
Pay or Appeal

Student pays online instantly or submits an appeal. Clerical staff reviews and resolves — all within the system.

User Portals

Built for Every Role

Dedicated portals designed for each type of user.

Student

Full transparency and control over your disciplinary records.

  • Register vehicles with grant
  • View all summons and status
  • Pay fines online securely
  • Submit and track appeals
Patrol Staff

Swift digital tools for on-site offense reporting.

  • Issue vehicle summons by plate
  • Issue misconduct summons by matric
  • Upload photo evidence
  • View personal report history
Clerical Staff

Administrative oversight with powerful monitoring tools.

  • Approve vehicle registrations
  • Process student appeals
  • Monitor student & patrol activity
  • View analytics and reports
